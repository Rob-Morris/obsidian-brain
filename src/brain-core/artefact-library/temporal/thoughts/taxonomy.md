# Thoughts

Temporal artefact. Raw, unformed thinking captured in the moment.

## Purpose

A thought is the precursor to an idea — rougher, more fleeting, possibly just a sentence or a question. Thoughts are temporal because they record what surfaced at a particular moment. The bar for capture is deliberately low: if something crosses your mind and feels worth noting, write it down. Most thoughts won't go anywhere, and that's fine. Some will spark ideas, writing, designs, or other artefacts.

## Graduation

A thought can become anything it makes sense to become. There is no prescribed path. When a thought does inspire a new artefact, use the provenance convention: add an origin line on the new artefact linking back to the thought, and add a spun-out callout on the thought linking forward.

## Naming

`yyyymmdd-thought--{slug}.md` in `_Temporal/Thoughts/yyyy-mm/`.

Example: `_Temporal/Thoughts/2026-03/20260320-thought--writing-as-thinking.md`

## Frontmatter

```yaml
---
type: temporal/thought
tags:
  - thought
---
```

## Trigger

When a raw thought surfaces during a session, capture it before it slips away.
