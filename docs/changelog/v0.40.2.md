# v0.40.2 - 2026-05-15

**Consolidate retrieval under `_search` and harden semantic refresh**

- **Consolidate the retrieval domain under `src/brain-core/scripts/_search/`.** `src/brain-core/scripts/_search/index.py`, `_search/query.py`, `_search/assets.py`, and `_search/lexical.py` now own lexical index construction, lexical/semantic/hybrid query behaviour, shared search-asset refresh, and lexical token/anchor helpers. `src/brain-core/scripts/_common/_search.py` is removed.
- **Reduce the old search scripts to staged wrappers over `_search`.** `src/brain-core/scripts/build_index.py` and `src/brain-core/scripts/search_index.py` are now thin script wrappers, while `src/brain-core/scripts/process.py`, `src/brain-core/scripts/construct_benchmark_fixture.py`, and `src/brain-core/scripts/evaluate_search.py` move their converged internal retrieval calls onto `_search`. Remaining MCP and lifecycle wrapper callers are explicitly deferred to later caller-convergence work.
- **Make semantic lifecycle refresh strict at the shared seam.** `src/brain-core/scripts/_search/assets.py` now distinguishes generic config-gated refresh from forced semantic refresh, and `src/brain-core/scripts/_semantic/provision.py` opts into `force_embeddings=True` so semantic provisioning/repair rebuilds sidecars or reports a structured failure instead of silently clearing them.
- **Describe and verify the staged boundary explicitly.** `tests/test_search_modules.py`, `tests/test_build_index.py`, `tests/test_search_index.py`, `tests/test_configure.py`, `tests/test_construct_benchmark_fixture.py`, `tests/test_evaluate_search.py`, and `tests/test_mcp_server.py` now cover the `_search` owners and the strict semantic-refresh contract directly, while `docs/functional/scripts.md`, `src/brain-core/scripts/README.md`, and `docs/architecture/overview.md` describe the remaining wrapper-era runtime surface honestly.
